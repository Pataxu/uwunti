UPCOMING FUR GUYS UPDATES

This page has been edited for the last time in 24 oct, 2020.

New logo.

The logo has been redesigned to match the new fox style.

Skin editor.

It's not beautiful, BUT it works. The coolest thing about it is that you can be the animal you want. Want to be a fox? Just paint yourself orange. Want to be a cat? Just be gray with pink ears. It's an early test, so there's no skin shop. Meanwhile, you will have 3 different eyes, 2 hats but 0 extra animals in your wardrobe.

Revamped physics.

Previously, physics were a buggy mess. And they still are! They've improved a bit, but you will still randomly teleport after being hit by something movable while you're above something movable, sorry! Also, foxes eyes change every time they get hit >_<

New levels.

2 new levels will appear the next update.

+ Extra.

- Countdown, Qualified and Eliminated texts are now 3D

- Foxes are now actual furries

- Player speed has been increased from 420 to 690

- Jump height and gravity increased. This means that jump height is the same as before, but you'll fall faster.

- Ads. Don't worry, they're not intrusive... at all.

- Goodbye BETA stage. The game will remain in beta, at least inside, but this update I will set the game as "released" instead of "open alpha" in Google Play so people can give their lovely 3 stars reviews.

- Fixed the "if I idle on here, I won't get hit by the cubes" bug.

- Fixed the "when I change my name and then the color, my name disappears" bug.

- Fixed the "I can put 'enter' spaces in the name and have multiple lines" bug.

- Level 1 has new visual features.

- Fixed the "when I change my name and then the color, my name disappears" bug.

- Movable platforms won't become static when you stop looking at them.
